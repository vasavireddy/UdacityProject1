About

Hello. Thanks for stopping by!

I'm Vasavi, and I do user experience design in San Francisco, CA.

For me, it's all about simplicity. I believe that designing simple and easy-to-use interfaces is the key to happy users.

Today, technology has become an integral part of our lives. With so many websites and apps competing for our attention, it is necessary to make them as simple and efficient as possible.

I never settle for less, and I am always trying to learn and improve. My goal as a designer is to create the best products with awesome user experiences.
