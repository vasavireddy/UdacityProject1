Process

Research

Before starting on a project, I make sure to invest a lot of time into user research as that feedback sets a good foundation from which I can design a product that actually solves users' needs.

Ideation

After performing my research, I focus on consolidating that user research and making meaning of everything I've learned. This consolidation involves sketching, wireframing and prototyping.

Iteration

Even after extensive research and ideation, I employ an iterative process in which I'll perform user testing on my prototypes and restart my design process to identify areas for improvement.

Reflection

Even after a project is complete, there are always lessons to be learned, and this period of reflection allows me to poke holes at my work and improve my process for the future.
